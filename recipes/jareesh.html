Jareesh

go back to the menu of recipes

Description

This is a saudi delicacy that is enjoyed

by families all over Saudi Arabia

for lunch and dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup White Rice
  • 1 1/2 Cups Jareesh
  • 1 Onion chopped
  • 1/4 Cup Oil
  • 4 Cups Water
  • 2 Cups Chicken broth or Veg
  • 2 Cups Yogurt
  • 2 Teaspoons Salt
  • 3 Onions chopped
  • 1/2 Cup Ghee
  • 1 Teaspoon Cumin

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and Jareesh till water is clear. Heat oil in a large pot, cook the onions till soft, add the rice and Jareesh and coat in the hot oil. Add the cinnamon and water then dissolve in the Knorr Professional Chicken Soup Stock.
  2. Cover with a lid and let it cook till all water is absorbed, about 2 hours, it will give you time to work on the all-important garnish which is essential to this recipe.
  3. Once the Jareesh has cooked stir in the yogurt, this is no easy task, you could use a cake mixer with a whisk attachment to make it easy, just dont overdo it a few seconds is enough.
