How do you make Kabsa?

Description

This is a saudi delicacy that is enjoyed

by families all over Saudi Arabia

for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 large Chicken, cut into 8 pieces with skin
  • 2 pieces Onions, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Sunflower oil
  • 0.5 tsp Ground cloves
  • 0.5 tsp Ground cinnamon
  • 0.5 tsp Ground cardamom

Steps

  1. In a large nonstick rice pot, saut√© onions in oil until golden. Add chicken pieces and cook for another 5 minutes until chicken is slightly golden.
  2. Add tomato paste, garlic, and spices and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer over medium low heat for 30 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.
  3. Add rice to the pot and gently stir, cover and simmer over low heat for 25 minutes or until the stock is absorbed and rice is fork tender. Transfer to a serving dish and serve.
